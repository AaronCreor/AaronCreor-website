Hello world.

The first time I wrote software, I was 15 years old in Grade 10, I didn't know it at the time, but software development would become my career and passion. At that time it was just an interesting elective for me, a class my friends were taking; I was lucky to have a teacher who would instill a love for technology in me. I remember my first program, in fact I still have the source code sitting in my Google Drive somewhere, just a simple print("Hello, World").

But to a teenager who had never coded before this was awesome, and something definitely clicked. I told my computer to do something and it did. It was fun bossing the computer around. Since then I've written plenty of code, some I'm genuinely proud of, and a lot that I'm not. Somewhere from then to now I realized that I didn't just like programming, I loved it.

So as "Hello, World" started my software development journey, I felt it only fitting that it start my blogging journey too.
